Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 7, 2025

1. Introduction

AddictingApps ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how "Where'd It Go" ("the App") handles user information.

2. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The App operates entirely on your device without transmitting data to our servers or third parties.

3. Permissions

The App requests access to the following device features:

  • Bluetooth: To detect and connect with Bluetooth-enabled items, facilitating the location of your lost belongings.
  • Camera: To assist in visually locating your items through augmented reality features.

These permissions are solely used for the App's core functionality. No data from these features is collected or transmitted.

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no personal information to secure. All operations are confined to your device, ensuring your privacy.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from users of any age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take appropriate action.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
